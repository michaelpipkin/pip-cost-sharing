About PipSplit

Hi there! My name is Michael Pipkin, and I am the solo developer behind PipSplit.

I created this site because I wanted an easy way to split monthly expenses with my housemate. From there, it grew into a fully-functional expense-sharing app. My goal is to offer a fast, reliable, and user-friendly tool for people who need to split costs among friends, roommates, or any group situation where shared expenses arise.

Our Mission

PipSplit helps you effortlessly track and split shared expenses with unlimited groups. Whether you're splitting costs for a weekend getaway with friends, tracking monthly expenses with roommates, or calculating everyone's share of a dinner out, PipSplit makes it simple and transparent. There's even an option to split an expense without creating an account!

What PipSplit Offers

  • Create and manage unlimited expense-sharing groups
  • Categorize expenses for better organization and analysis
  • View detailed summaries showing who owes whom
  • Save frequently used expenses as templates for quick entry
  • Track payment history and expense details over time
  • Split individual expenses without creating an account

My Values

As a developer, I am committed to continuous improvement and listening to user feedback. PipSplit is a passion project of mine, and I believe that expense sharing should be simple, transparent, and accessible to everyone. I'm dedicated to keeping this tool free, fast, and focused on what matters most: making shared expenses stress-free.
